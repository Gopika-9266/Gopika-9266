### 🧭 About Me

I'm a final-year **AI & Machine Learning undergraduate** at Saveetha Engineering College (CGPA 8.3, graduating 2026) who got hooked on a specific question: *how do you take messy, raw data and turn it into something a business can actually trust and use?*

That question is why I keep building **ETL pipelines** instead of just notebooks. During my internship at Arjun Vision Tech Solutions, I processed e-commerce datasets and built pipelines to organize raw data for reporting — and the part I enjoyed most wasn't the model, it was making sure the *data feeding the model* was clean, consistent, and automated. Since then I've built pipelines with **Apache Airflow**, **PostgreSQL**, and **MySQL** to scratch that same itch on my own projects.

I'm equally comfortable on the ML side (I built a forensic face-sketch matching system using Siamese Neural Networks), but my career direction is clear: I want to grow into a **Data Engineer** role — someone who builds the reliable pipelines and infrastructure that data teams depend on.

- 🔭 Currently sharpening my data engineering fundamentals: orchestration, data modeling, and cloud-based pipelines
- 🌱 Learning cloud data platforms (AWS/GCP) and dbt to round out my ETL skill set
- 🤝 Open to internships and entry-level roles in **Data Engineering**, **Data Analytics**, or **AI/ML with a data focus**
- 💬 Ask me about ETL pipelines, SQL, or how I matched forensic face sketches with neural networks
- ⚡ Fun fact: I once led a meme-making event at my college fest — turns out organizing chaos isn't that different whether it's memes or messy datasets
